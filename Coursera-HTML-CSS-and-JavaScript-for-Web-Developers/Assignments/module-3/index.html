Beef

Sushi

A sushi menu typically features a variety of rolls, sashimi, and nigiri made with fresh fish, vegetables, and seasoned rice. Classic options include tuna, salmon, and eel, while specialty rolls may incorporate unique flavors like spicy mayo or tempura. Vegetarian rolls often use avocado, cucumber, or sweet potato. Side dishes like miso soup, edamame, and seaweed salad complement the meal. Sauces like soy sauce, wasabi, and pickled ginger enhance the sushi's flavor. The menu may also include omakase, where the chef selects a personalized assortment.(Back to Top)
